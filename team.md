# Meet the Superstars Team
{: .no_toc }

Our team brings together expertise in marine biology, computational biology, and bioinformatics to investigate protein evolution in early-branching animals.

---

## Akili

**Role:** Coral Immunologist and Bioinformatition 

Akili is a graduate researcher specializing in coral cell immunology and -omics bioinformatics pipline tools. With a background in both computer science and molecular biology, Akili and the other superstars focus on identifying and characterizing protease gene families in non-model marine organisms, using tools like Biopython to automate large-scale sequence analyses. Outside of research, Akili is passionate about making bioinformatics more accessible through open, reproducible science.
